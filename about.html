About me

I'm Justin, a former-homeschooler and junior undergrad studying Computer Science at Mahidol University International College.

Homeschooled?!

Some people see this as a good thing, some people see it as bad. To me it's a blend of both. Thanks to homeschooling:

  • I'm self-disciplined thanks to managing my own time and teaching myself
  • I'm self-motivated and learn because I want to, no one ever forced me
  • I can learn anything by myself

But, naturally of course, it came with some disadvantages:

  • My 'curriculum' was not as structured as high school classes, which led to me struggling a bit in some first-year courses
  • Having never been subject to constant examination, I am more stress-sensitive during exam periods

However, I see the overall experience as a positive one.
